## About The Workshop

This workshop featured four 2-hour sessions and covered the basics of web development. They were designed for adults with very little to no experience coding and were offered for free at the local library.

Accross the four weeks, participants were introduced to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. They engaged in hands on activities and built projects, leaving them with the foundations to pursue further learning at the end of the workshop.
